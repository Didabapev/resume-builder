In 2026, the ideal resume length for most professionals is one page. Recruiters prefer concise resumes that quickly highlight value and achievements. If you have less than 10 years of experience, keep it to one page. Senior professionals with extensive achievements may use two pages, but every line must add value. Remove outdated jobs, irrelevant skills, and repetitive descriptions. Focus on results rather than responsibilities. Remember: quality beats quantity. A focused one-page resume often performs better than a long, unfocused document.
