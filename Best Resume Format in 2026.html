Choosing the best resume format in 2026 is critical if you want to pass ATS systems and impress recruiters. The most recommended format today is the reverse-chronological resume. This structure highlights your most recent job experience first and clearly shows your career growth. Recruiters typically spend less than 10 seconds scanning a resume. A clean structure with clear headings like Summary, Experience, Skills, and Education helps them quickly find relevant information. Functional resumes are less popular because they hide work history, which many employers consider a red flag. Use a professional font, avoid graphics, and keep formatting simple. ATS systems scan keywords from job descriptions, so always tailor your resume to match the position you’re applying for. If you’re unsure where to start, use an online resume builder to generate a properly structured document instantly.
