Best Resume Fonts in 2026

Choosing the right font improves readability, professionalism, and ATS compatibility. A clean, simple font ensures recruiters can easily scan your resume.

Top Professional Resume Fonts

  • Arial – Clean and modern
  • Calibri – Professional and easy to read
  • Times New Roman – Traditional and formal
  • Helvetica – Modern and elegant
  • Georgia – Classic and readable

Recommended Font Size

Use 10–12 points for body text and 14–16 points for section headings. Avoid very small or oversized fonts.

Fonts to Avoid

  • Script fonts
  • Decorative fonts
  • Overly stylized typography

Keep Formatting Consistent

Use one or two fonts maximum. Maintain consistent spacing, alignment, and section structure for a polished look.

Final Advice

A simple, ATS-friendly font ensures your resume looks professional and passes automated screening systems.
