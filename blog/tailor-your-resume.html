Best Skills to Put on a Resume in 2026

Choosing the right skills can significantly increase your chances of landing interviews. Employers look for a mix of hard and soft skills that match the job requirements.

Hard Skills

Hard skills are technical abilities that can be measured and tested.

  • Programming (Python, JavaScript)
  • Data Analysis
  • Graphic Design
  • Project Management
  • Digital Marketing
  • Accounting

Soft Skills

Soft skills show how you work and interact with others.

  • Communication
  • Leadership
  • Problem-Solving
  • Time Management
  • Adaptability
  • Teamwork

Match Skills to the Job

Always tailor your skills section to the job description. Use relevant keywords to improve your chances of passing Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Quality Over Quantity

Avoid listing too many unrelated skills. Focus on the most impactful abilities that align with your career goals.

Final Tip

Support your skills with achievements in your work experience section to make your resume stronger and more credible.
