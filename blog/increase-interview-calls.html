How to Increase Interview Calls with Your Resume in 2026

To increase interview calls, tailor each resume to the specific job description. Generic resumes often fail to pass Applicant Tracking Systems.

Optimize Keywords

Carefully read the job posting and include relevant keywords naturally throughout your resume.

Highlight Measurable Results

Use numbers and data to demonstrate impact. For example, “Increased sales by 25%” or “Reduced costs by 15%.”

Apply Consistently

Success often comes from consistent applications. Track your submissions and adjust your strategy when needed.

Update Based on Feedback

Small improvements in formatting, clarity, and achievements can significantly improve response rates.
