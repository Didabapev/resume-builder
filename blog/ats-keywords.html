ATS Keywords Guide: How to Beat the Robots in 2026

Did you know that 75% of resumes are never seen by a real person? They are filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). To survive, your resume needs the right keywords.

What are ATS Keywords?

Keywords are specific words or phrases that recruiters use to search for candidates. They usually include job titles, hard skills, software names, and industry certifications.

Example for a Marketing Manager:
SEO, Content Strategy, Google Analytics, Social Media ROI, HubSpot, Campaign Management.

How to Find Keywords for Your Resume

  • Analyze the Job Description: Look for words that appear more than once.
  • Industry Standards: Use common terms (e.g., use "Project Management" instead of "Leading Tasks").
  • Software & Tools: Always list specific tools you know (Excel, Jira, Figma, etc.).

Where to Place Them?

Don't just hide keywords in white text at the bottom! Place them naturally in your Professional Summary, Skills Section, and Work Experience.
