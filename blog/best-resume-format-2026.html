Best Resume Format in 2026

Choosing the best resume format in 2026 is essential if you want to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and impress hiring managers. Recruiters spend less than 10 seconds scanning a resume, so your layout must be clear and easy to read.

1. Reverse-Chronological Resume (Recommended)

The reverse-chronological format is the most popular and ATS-friendly resume format. It lists your most recent job first and clearly shows your career growth.

2. Functional Resume

This format focuses on skills instead of work history. However, many recruiters dislike it because it hides employment gaps.

3. Combination Resume

The hybrid format mixes skills and work experience. It works well for professionals with strong expertise.

Why Reverse-Chronological is Best in 2026

ATS systems scan resumes for keywords and structure. The reverse format makes it easier for software and recruiters to quickly understand your background.

Final Tips

  • Use clear section headings
  • Avoid graphics and complex tables
  • Keep formatting simple
  • Tailor your resume for each job
