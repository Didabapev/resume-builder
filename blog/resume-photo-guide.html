Should You Add a Photo to Your Resume in 2026?

In the US job market, adding a photo to your resume is not recommended. It may create bias and is often rejected by ATS systems.

When a Photo Is NOT Recommended

In countries like the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada, resumes typically do not include photos.

When a Photo Is Acceptable

In some European and Asian countries, adding a professional photo may be standard practice.

ATS Considerations

Applicant Tracking Systems may misread resumes that contain images. A photo can reduce compatibility.

Final Recommendation

If you are unsure, leave the photo out. Focus on your skills and achievements instead.
