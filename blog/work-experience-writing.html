Your work experience section is the most important part of your resume. Start each bullet point with a strong action verb like “managed,” “developed,” or “implemented.” Focus on measurable results. Instead of saying “worked on marketing campaigns,” write “launched marketing campaign that increased website traffic by 40%.” Keep bullet points concise and relevant to the job. Limit each role to 3–5 strong achievements. This approach makes your resume powerful and results-driven.
