Even qualified candidates get rejected due to simple resume mistakes. Spelling errors, inconsistent formatting, and generic descriptions can harm your chances. Avoid copying the same resume for every job. Tailoring your resume shows effort and increases ATS compatibility. Another mistake is listing responsibilities without achievements. Employers care about results. Using unprofessional email addresses or including irrelevant personal details can also reduce credibility. Always proofread your resume and ask someone to review it before applying.
