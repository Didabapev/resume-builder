How to List Education on a Resume in 2026

Your education section should be clear, concise, and structured. Employers want to quickly see your degree, institution, and graduation year.

Start with Your Highest Degree

Always list your most recent or highest degree first. Include:

  • Degree name (e.g., Bachelor of Science in Computer Science)
  • Institution name
  • Graduation year
  • Location (optional)

Include Relevant Details (If Needed)

If you are a recent graduate, you can also include:

  • Relevant coursework
  • Academic achievements
  • Honors or awards
  • GPA (if strong)

Where to Place Education on Your Resume

Students and entry-level candidates usually place education near the top. Experienced professionals place it after work experience.

Keep It Concise

Avoid listing outdated or irrelevant education details. Keep formatting clean and consistent.

Final Tip

A well-organized education section helps recruiters quickly understand your academic background and qualifications.
