How to Write a Cover Letter in 2026

A cover letter complements your resume by explaining why you are a strong candidate for the position. A well-written cover letter can significantly increase your chances of getting an interview.

1. Start with a Strong Opening

Address the hiring manager by name if possible. Clearly state the position you’re applying for and express enthusiasm for the role.

2. Highlight Relevant Achievements

Focus on 1–2 key accomplishments that directly relate to the job. Quantify results whenever possible.

3. Show How You Add Value

Explain how your skills and experience can benefit the company. Demonstrate that you understand their needs.

4. Keep It Concise

Your cover letter should be no longer than one page. Avoid repeating your resume word-for-word.

5. End with a Clear Call to Action

Close professionally and express your interest in discussing the opportunity further.

Final Advice

Customize your cover letter for every job application. A tailored letter shows genuine interest and effort.
